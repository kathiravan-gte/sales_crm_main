How to Create a Lead

Add new potential customers to the pipeline

  1. 1

    Navigate to the Leads page

    Click Leads in the left sidebar or go to /leads.

  2. 2

    Click "Add Lead"

    The blue Add Lead button is in the top-right of the toolbar. A modal dialog will appear.

  3. 3

    Fill in the lead details

    Required: First Name, Last Name, Email. Optional: Phone, Company, Status.

  4. 4

    Click "Create Lead"

    The lead is saved and an activity is automatically logged. It will appear at the top of the table.

Tip: You can also import leads in bulk using a CSV file via the API endpoint /api/public/lead.

How to Convert a Lead to a Deal

Turn a qualified lead into an active deal opportunity

  1. 1

    Open the Lead Detail page

    Click the eye icon on any lead row, or click the lead's name to open their detail view.

  2. 2

    Click "Convert to Deal"

    The green button in the top-right opens the conversion modal. Only visible if the lead is not already converted.

  3. 3

    Enter the Opportunity Name and Expected Revenue

    The deal name defaults to the lead's name. Set the expected deal value in dollars.

  4. 4

    Click "Create Deal"

    The lead is marked as Converted, a contact is automatically created, and the deal enters the New stage in the pipeline.

How to Use the Dashboard

Monitor your pipeline health and team performance

Main Dashboard /

  • KPI cards: Total Leads, Active Deals, Won Deals, Tasks, Meetings
  • Recent activities and meetings at a glance

  • AI Meeting Intelligence: paste transcripts to auto-extract tasks

Reports /dashboard

  • Pipeline by Stage: deal values per stage
  • AI Insights: auto-generated observations from your data

How to Log Activities

Track every call, email, meeting, and task

  1. 1

    Go to Activities /activities

    Click Activities in the sidebar.

  2. 2

    Click "Log Activity"

    A modal appears. Fill in the Title (required), Type (Call/Email/Meeting/Task), and Status.

  3. 3

    Link to a Lead or Deal (optional)

    Use the dropdowns to associate the activity with an existing lead or deal for full history tracking.

  4. 4

    Mark activities complete

    Pending activities show a Mark done link. Click it to move the activity to completed status.
